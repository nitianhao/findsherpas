Industry Context

How You Compare

{% for b in benchmarks %} {% endfor %}
Metric Your Score Industry Target Source
{{ b.metric }} {{ b.your_score }} {{ b.target }} {{ b.source }}

70% of ecommerce sites cannot handle typos in their site search (Baymard Institute). Sites that do handle them gain a significant edge.

~30% of ecommerce visitors use site search, but they generate a disproportionate share of revenue — search users convert at 1.8–3x the rate of browse-only visitors (Econsultancy, Algolia).

31% of site searches end at a dead end — irrelevant results or zero results — across the average ecommerce site (Baymard Institute).

The top 3 results capture ~55–60% of all clicks. Results below position 3 are seen by a rapidly shrinking share of customers.

Scope and Boundaries

What this audit covers

Included

Search relevance, ranking quality, retrieval gaps, query interpretation failures, visible result ordering, and prioritized fixes based on the tested query set.

Not included

Implementation work, platform configuration changes, merchandising strategy, analytics instrumentation, or a full conversion-rate analysis beyond the search result experience.
